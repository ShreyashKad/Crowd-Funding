VISION AND MISSION

MISSION

  • To develop and strengthen ties between our Alumni and the Department of Chemistry by providing diverse tangible benefits including career services, networking opportunities, special events and lectures, and the opportunity to connect with and inspire students and graduates. And, to engage Alumni with Departmental research and future goals, in order that each individual feels welcome and valued as a member of the Thakur Alumini community.

    • VISION

    1. A respectful, congenial, and equitable programme that is welcoming and engaging for alumni of all ages, as well as their partners, families, and guests.
    2. A dynamic community of active alumni who contribute to the goals of the programme, feel ownership over its direction, and act as ambassadors for Chemistry at Oxford.
    3. A programme with strong ties to industry and spin-out companies, and the ability of promote mentoring, internship and career opportunities for students and alumni in this and other fields.
    4. Accessible and innovative promotion of the Department’s research in order to connect alumni and the public to cutting edge science.
